3 weeks in Shenzhen

Work in the world's biggest hardware cluster, fully focus on your project.

    Arrival in Hong Kong

    Spend 2 days in Hong Kong, China's main shipping and finance hub. While we wait for visas to process, you get to know the rest of the group and their projects.

  • Nov 4 and 5

    Factory tours in Shenzhen

    Check-in in Shenzhen. Tour a selection of our best factories in the area.

  • Nov 6

    Networking with local hardware startups

    Meet and connect with selected tech startups from the local incubator.

  • Nov 6 – 20

    Prototyping

    Refine and prototype your product together with the factory and local engineers.

  • Nov 10 – 12

    Trip to Suzhou

    Visit China's second big technology cluster.

  • Nov 30

    Final presentations

    Present your progress to the rest of the team. Find common challenges.

  • After Nov 30

    Organize sourcing and manufacturing

    When you're ready, we will find the best supplier to mass-produce the first batch of your product. We will also assist you in certification, exporting and shipping.
