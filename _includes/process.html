The Process

Prototype and manufacture your hardware ideas together with other entrepreneurs.

Prototype

You have an idea for an awesome hardware product or a finished prototype. Together with your fellow makers and experienced factory engineers you work on perfecting your product or adapt something existing.

Manufacture

Ready to push out the first batch? We find you a suitable factory and negotiate the best deal.

Bring to market

Selling in the US or Europe? We will guide you through the certification process and handle logistics. Lean back and focus on marketing and product development.
